Rules Quick View

  • PvE is protected: no killing outside Extraction/KOS zones.
  • No griefing in PvE, Community Center, or other players' safehouses.
  • Do not claim non-residential buildings or move airdrop crates.
  • Safehouse limits: max 8 vehicles and 10 animals.
  • Floor loot can be auto-deleted. Store items in containers.
